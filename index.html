What is Educational Technology?

Educational Technology is the use of apps, technology devices, and educational theory to assist in the development and communication of knowledge.

Welcome to my Ed Tech website! This website is an ongoing project to highlight the power of integrating technology in a K-5 classroom setting and how I empower teachers to use educational technology tools in their classrooms.
