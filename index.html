Favorite Ed Tech Tools

  • Flipgrid- Flipgrid is a tool that allows learners to record videos in response to a question or a prompt. It gives students a voice and a way to express themselves.
  • Padlet- Padlet is another tool that gives students a voice. When learners post on a Padlet, they can respond using text, video, voice, and/or pictures. Learners can also collaborate with others through replies.
  • Nearpod- Nearpod has the potentional to make learning interactive. It is a great tool to keep students engaged, formatively assess their understanding, and give them a voice.
  • Google Jamboard- Google Jamboard allows learners the opportunity to collaborate in real time, show their thinking, and express their thoughts.
